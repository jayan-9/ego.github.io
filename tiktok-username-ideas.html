✨ How to Make Them YOURS

If you see a name you like but it's already taken, try these simple tricks:

1. Add an underscore or dot: @Couch.Conspiracy_ or @Couch.Conspiracy.exe
2. Add a number with meaning: @NotUrAvgMochi (use your birth year or lucky number like 23, 07, 99)
3. Use "its" or "the": @ItsThePickleRick or @TheCouchConspiracy
4. Add "TV" or "Tok": @PickleRickTok or @CouchConspiracyTV
5. Add your initials: @CR_CouchConspiracy or @PickleRick_AB

You can customize these names according to your style and make them uniquely yours!
